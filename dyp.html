DETACHED YOUTH WORK

Detached youth work is at the core of our work, helping us reach out and engage sometimes challenging and marginalized young people. We have youth workers out on the streets in Consett and the surrounding areas 5 nights a week. Youth workers meet young people where they are at in parks and streets and develop meaningful and trusting relationships. This is often the starting point for young people accessing our other services.

CENTRE BASED SESSIONS

Derwentside Youth Project also recognise and appreciate that there is a need for young people to engage in centre based sessions. Qualified Youth Workers run 2 hour drop in sessions at various locations throughout the derwentside area over several evenings of the week. These venues provide safe, comfortable, secure environments for young people to meet up, socialise and receive appropriate information and advice as and when required. Young people engaged in these centres will be offered opportunities to attend activities and participate in workshops as well as signposting to relevant external services.
